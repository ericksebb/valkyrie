Valkyrie

A valkyrie is one of a host of female figures who guide souls of the dead to the god Odin's hall Valhalla. There, the deceased warriors become einherjar.
They are sometimes described as the daughters of royalty, sometimes accompanied by ravens and sometimes connected to swans or horses.

"Countless winters, we served the Allfather. But only through his union to the Queen, did we ever taste some measure of freedom" - Sigrun, God of War: Ragnarok (2022)
